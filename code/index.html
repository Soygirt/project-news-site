Man sees troll!

It was a late afternoon when Alf Simonsson was walking his dog. He heard a strange noise in the woods close to the gravel road and went to investigate. When he had walked 200 meters into the forest he heard the noise again. His dog started barking at what he first thought was a rock but when the rock started moving he saw that it was in fact a troll! Haven't heard of trolls? Click this link to see some Google results.
